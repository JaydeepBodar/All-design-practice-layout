Porcelain Paving

Porcelain Paving is a great choice for contemporary designs and offers non-slip, low-maintenance and scratch-resistant properties. Each porcelain tile is designed to mimic the finish of natural stone whilst providing all the modern convenience of a state-of-the-art product!

The consistent colours and textures make porcelain paving a great choice with options suitable for any design, giving you full control over your project! Choose from a vast range of colours from striking Black and Nero options to the ever-popular Silver-Grey Porcelain, as well as light and bright options, like our White Porcelain.

As a low-maintenance option, you won’t need to worry about any staining, spills or algae, saving you time and money! Our Sandstone Paving prices start at just £27.72/m² Incl. VAT!

Granite Paving

Granite Paving features a lightly flamed or shot-blasted surface texture which helps the stone to retain its non-slip properties underfoot, even when wet!

As one of the most robust types of patio paving slabs available, Granite paving offers a scratch-resistant surface and the durability to withstand plenty of use, ensuring that your paving stays looking its best all year round! The hard-wearing garden slabs are designed for outdoor use and will stand the test of time even in harsh weather.

Choose from Blue Black and Silver Grey to something more striking, such as Pink or Black options.
